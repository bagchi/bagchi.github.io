Reading List for Dependable Computing and Fault Tolerance

Saurabh Bagchi

Purdue University

Last update: July 17, 2016

 

This reading list is appropriate for a graduate level course. This is a superset of what can be read and discussed in a semester long course.

If you use this reading list, please drop me a note by clicking here.

 

Foundational papers

[1]  Reibman & M. Veeraraghavan, (Bell Labs) "Reliability modeling: an overview for system designers," Computer, Vol. 24, No. 4, April 1991, pp. 49-57.

[2]  Schlichting & Schneider, "Fail-stop processors: an approach to designing fault-tolerant computing systems," ACM Trans. Comp. Sys., v 1, pp 222-238, Aug. 1983.

[3]  Abraham, J., & Siewiorek, D., "An algorithm for the accurate reliability evaluation of triple modular redundancy networks," IEEE Trans. Computers, July 1974

 

Terminology

[4]  Avizienis, Algirdas, J-C. Laprie, Brian Randell, and Carl Landwehr. "Basic concepts and taxonomy of dependable and secure computing." IEEE transactions on dependable and secure computing 1, no. 1 (2004): 11-33.

 

Hardware fault tolerance

[5]  A. Mahmood, E.J. Mccluskey, and D.J. Lu, Concurrent Fault Detection Using a Watchdog Processor and Assertions, Proceedings 13th IEEE Int. Test Conference, 622-628, 1983.

[6]  W. Cheng, J. Patel, Concurrent Error Detection in Iterative Logic Arrays, Proceedings 14th Int. Symp. on Fault-Tolerant Computing, 1984, pp. 10-15.

[7]  P. M. Chen, E. K. Lee, G. A. Gibson, et al., RAID: High-Performance, Reliable Secondary Storage, ACM Computing Surveys, vol. 26, pp. 145-185, 1994.

[8]  Z. Alkhalifa, V.S.S. Nair, N. Krishnamurthy, and J.A. Abraham, Design and Evaluation of System-Level Checks for On-Line Control Flow Error Detection, IEEE Trans. on Parallel and Distributed Systems, vol. 10, no. 6, 627-641, June 1999.

[9]  N. Nakka, K. Pattabiraman, and R. Iyer, Processor-Level Selective Replication, in Proceedings of the 37th Annual IEEE/IFIP International Conference on Dependable Systems and Networks (DSN). 2007.

 

Software error detection

[10]         Huang, Kuang-Hua, and Jacob A. Abraham. "Algorithm-based fault tolerance for matrix operations." IEEE transactions on computers 100, no. 6 (1984): 518-528.

[11]         Liming Chen, A. Avizienis, N-version Programming: A Fault-Tolerance Approach to Reliability Of Software Operation, In Proceedings of the Twenty-Fifth International Symposium on Fault-Tolerant Computing, 1995, pp.113-119, 27-30 Jun 1995.

[12]         Chen, Wei, Sam Toueg, and Marcos Kawazoe Aguilera. "On the quality of service of failure detectors." IEEE Transactions on computers 51, no. 5 (2002): 561-580.

[13]         S. Bagchi, B. Srinivasan, K. Whisnant, Z. Kalbarczyk, and R. K. Iyer, "Hierarchical error detection in a software implemented fault tolerance (SIFT) environment," Knowledge and Data Engineering, IEEE Transactions on , vol.12, no.2, pp.203, 224, March/April 2000.

[14]         K. Pattabiraman, G. P. Saggese, D. Chen, Z. Kalbarczyk, R. Iyer, "Automated Derivation of Application-Specific Error Detectors Using Dynamic Analysis," Dependable and Secure Computing, IEEE Transactions on , vol.8, no.5, pp.640-655, Sept.-Oct. 2011.

 

Software engineering and fault tolerance

[15]         J. P. Black, D. J. Taylor, and D. E. Morgan, An Introduction to Robust Data Structures, Digest of Papers, 10th Int. Symposium on Fault-Tolerant Computing, 110-112, 1980.

[16]         Ernst, Michael D., Jake Cockrell, William G. Griswold, and David Notkin. "Dynamically discovering likely program invariants to support program evolution." IEEE Transactions on Software Engineering 27, no. 2 (2001): 99-123.

[17]         D. Dhurjati and V. Adve. Backwards-Compatible Array Bounds Checking for C with Very Low Overhead, In Proceedings of the 28th International Conference in Software Engineering, Shanghai, China, May 2006.

 

Fault injection based evaluation

[18]         Stott, David T., Greg Ries, Mei-Chen Hsueh, and Ravishankar K. Iyer. "Dependability analysis of a high-speed network using software-implemented fault injection and simulated fault injection." IEEE Transactions on Computers 47, no. 1 (1998): 108-119.

[19]         M. Cukier, R. Chandra, D. Henke, J. Pistole, and W. H. Sanders, Fault injection based on a partial view of the global state of a distributed system, in Proceedings of the 18th Symposium on Reliable Distributed Systems (SRDS99), pp. 168177, October 1999.

[20]         H. Madeira, M. Vieira, and D. Costa, On the Emulation of Software Faults by Software Fault Injection, International Conference on Dependable Systems and Networks, New York, USA, pp. 417-426, June 2000.

[21]         Pattabiraman, Karthik, Nithin M. Nakka, Zbigniew T. Kalbarczyk, and Ravishankar K. Iyer. "Symplfied: Symbolic program-level fault injection and error detection framework." IEEE Transactions on Computers 62, no. 11 (2013): 2292-2307.

 

 

Experimental data analysis

[22]         Chandra, Subhash, and Peter M. Chen. "How fail-stop are faulty programs?." In Fault-Tolerant Computing, 1998. Digest of Papers. Twenty-Eighth Annual International Symposium on, pp. 240-249. IEEE, 1998.

[23]         Albinet, Arnaud, Jean Arlat, and J-C. Fabre. "Characterization of the Impact of Faulty Drivers on the Robustness of the Linux Kernel." In Dependable Systems and Networks, 2004 International Conference on, pp. 867-876. IEEE, 2004.

 

Case studies

[24]         N. G. Leveson, et al., The Use of Self Checks and Voting in Software Error Detection: An Empirical Study. IEEE Transactions on Software Engineering, 16(4): p. 432-443, 1990.

[25]         S. Bagchi, Y. Liu, K. Whisnant, Z. Kalbarczyk, R. Iyer, A Framework for Database Audit and Control Flow Checking for a Wireless Telephone Network Controller. In Proceedings of the 2001 International Conference on Dependable Systems and Networks (DSN), Goteborg, pp. 225-234, 2001.

[26]         Wang, Nicholas J., Justin Quek, Todd M. Rafacz, and Sanjay J. Patel. "Characterizing the effects of transient faults on a high-performance processor pipeline." In Dependable Systems and Networks, 2004 International Conference on, pp. 61-70. IEEE, 2004.
