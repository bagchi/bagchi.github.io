Fall 2004 Group Champ Competition

Earn points for activities during the semester. The one with the maximum number of points at the end of the semester gets the “Group Champ” award with a certificate and a plaque.

Points for conference or journal paper: 3 (Submitted), 5 (Accepted); Proposal: 3 (Submitted), 2 (Accepted); Presentations: 4 (External), 3 (Internal); Mentoring new members: 2; Departmental award: 2 (Applied), 3 (Won); Special initiatives: 2.

Ordering Materials

Send me an email with the item you want to purchase. If you do not hear back from me within 7 days, that implies you have the go-ahead.

Fill out “Materials Requested By”, “Vendor Suggested”, “Materials to be used for”, “Authorized by”, “Item description”.

Take it to Phyllis Smith in Business Office and

Either check out the department credit card and buy it directly from the vendor

Or, have her place the order for the item

Send an email to Carol and me with description of item purchased

Typical items to be purchased through this means: books, stationery, manuals, conference proceedings

Purchase limit is $100. For higher purchases, let Carol handle it.

Food in the Lab and Cleanliness

A fridge and a microwave for all the users together with coffee and drinks. There will be a rotating Provision Chair with a period of 3 months who is responsible for keeping it stocked. When it runs out, buy the necessary stuff and give me the bill.

The condition for the use of the facilities is to keep the lab clean. The lab is to be cleaned every week according to the schedule below. The Provision Chair will draw up the schedule. If you use the fridge, keep your food labeled with your name. Cleaning implies sweeping the floors, cleaning the food cabinets, the fridge, and the microwave. The cleaning supplies are to be taken from the janitorial staff.
