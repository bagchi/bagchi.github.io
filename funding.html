Funding in Dependable Computing Systems Lab

Our funding comes from the following sources:

1.      National Science Foundation

                                 i.      Sensors program, Electrical and Communication Systems (ECS), Directorate for Engineering (ENG)

                               ii.      Computer Systems Research (CSR) program, Embedded and Hybrid Systems (EHS), Computer and Information Science and Engineering (CISE)

2.      Indiana 21st Century Research & Technology Fund – Round 6 project on “Detection and Control of Combined Sewer Overflow Events Using Embedded Sensor Network Technology” with U. of Notre Dame.

3.      Purdue Research Foundation – Research grant on “Tolerating malicious and natural failures in distributed applications through non-intrusive mechanisms”.

4.      Intel – Sensor network testbed

5.      Avaya – Voice over IP equipment
